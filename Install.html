QR SESSION

  • ➤
    Click on "Run on Repl.it" button.  
run on repl.it
  • ➤
    Now you will get a QR code. Scan it from your Whatsapp Web.
  • ➤
     Open WhatsApp > Linked devices > Link A device > Scan QR code
  • ➤
    You will now receive your session code on your login number. (Check messages in your own WhatsApp account)
  • ➤
    In the next step (when depositing) remember (copy and paste) that code as your AQUA_SESSION.

DEPLOY AQUABOT


  • ➤
     Click on the" Deploy to heroku "button. After clicking on it, read the form carefully and fill up the information relevant to activating your bot.
deploy to heroku
  • ➤
    When you have finished filling out the form, click the "Deploy App" button at the bottom of the form.
  • ➤
    Wait a few minutes until you get four green right marks.
  • ➤
    When you get four green marks, click on the "Manage app" button.
  • ➤
    You will now see Heroku settings and resources that allow you to control the bot from Heroku.
  • ➤
    Now go to the Resources tab in Heroku.
  • ➤
    Click on the pencil-like icon and keep the switch on and confirm
  • ➤
    Then, click the "More" button and go to "View logs".
  • ➤
    You will see a log of bot. Wait until you received "Loading EN/SI language..."
  • ➤
    Now you can use your own Aqua.
